A Letter For You

From my heart to yours

July 2026

My dearest Sara,

There are a thousand ways I could begin this letter, and I've probably written it a hundred times in my head — but nothing I imagine quite captures what I feel when I think about you.

You are the kind of person who makes ordinary moments feel extraordinary. The way you laugh, the way you see the world, the way you've somehow made every day brighter just by being in it — I notice all of it. I cherish all of it.

I built this little space for you because you deserve to know how much you mean to me. Not just in texts or quick moments, but in something that lasts. Something you can come back to whenever you need a reminder that you are deeply, completely loved.

Every photo here, every memory, every silly game — it's all for you. Because you are worth every effort and then some.

I love you, Sara. More than I know how to say — so I made a whole website instead. 💕

Always yours,
Bilal
