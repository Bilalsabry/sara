My dearest Sara,

There are a thousand ways I could begin this, and I've rewritten it in my head more times than I can count. But the truth is simple: you are the kind of person who makes ordinary moments feel like they matter. And that's rare.

The way you carry yourself. The way you think. The quiet strength in how you see the world — I notice all of it, and I don't take any of it for granted.

I built this little place for you because you deserve more than a text. You deserve something that stays, something you can return to on any ordinary Tuesday when you need to be reminded that you are seen, and known, and completely loved.

Every photo here, every line, every memory — it belongs to you. Because you do.

Always yours, Bilal
